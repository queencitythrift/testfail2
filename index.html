About

Queen City Thrift is a reselling business run by Katie Neeb where you can buy high quality thrifted clothes. QCT functions completely through Instagram, with everything from item previews and bidding and was started in Katie's hometown of Cinncinati, Ohio and has recently moved to Boston where she attends college.

How does it work?

Once a drop of a couple similar items has been put together, a post will go up with the entire set announcing the date of release and other information Then each item will get its own post describing the item and setting a buy it now and starting bid price. If you would like to purchase for the BIN you can comment or you can wait for the auction to start and make your bids then. If you win the item you will message your location and venmo the amount bidded and await your new clothes!
